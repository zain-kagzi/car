OUR STORY

About
Apex Motors

Since 2010, we've been at the cutting edge of automotive performance and engineering excellence.

MISSION

Engineering the Future,
One Car at a Time

Apex Motors was founded with one obsession: to create automobiles that don't just transport — they transform. Every Apex vehicle is an expression of mechanical artistry, blending aerospace technology with visceral performance.

Our team of 200+ engineers, designers, and aerodynamicists work from our state-of-the-art facility, where every component is hand-inspected before reaching the chassis.

From our debut GT-X coupe to our all-electric Apex Storm concept, we've always been at the frontier. Our racing division has competed in 40+ international endurance events, bringing track lessons into every road car.
